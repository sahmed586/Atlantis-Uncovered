History of Atlantis Uncovered

With the recent breakthrough discovery of the lost city of Atlantis about 10 years ago, we have constructed this theme park directly above Atlantis in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean! Our archaelogists have worked long and hard to identify the lost city, observe it, and test how the civilization used to function. Now how exacly does this relate to your Atlantis Uncovered experience you may ask? Not only is our entire theme park Atlantis themed and based on phenomenal infrastructure, but we have our signature water ride known as the Atlantis Dipper that will take you directly down to see the legendary lost city with your own eyes! Through a transparent water slide woven throughout the palace guided by out knowledgable tour guides, this is guaranteed to be an experience unlike any other!



Parking and Location

Knowing that the amusement park is based in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean, you may be wondering how atransportation and parking may be imapcted. Here at Atlantis Uncovered, we three bases on the east coast where our customers can come and park. You will then aboard the ferry which will shuttle you over to the island! Parking and ferry fees are included within your ticket purchase. To the left you can view the map of our coastal ports and the location of the island.



Concessions

We know we have people from all around the world arriving to our park with various dietary restrictions! That being said, we accommmodate to those needs and provide a variety of food types. We have your classic American festival foods, snacka and desserts from corn dogs to elephant ears and everything in between but we also provide vegeterian, kosher, and halal options! As for beverages, we have our famous selection of lemonades, pepsi products and fresh juices!
